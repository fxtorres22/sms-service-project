SMS Service Project

Operated by Fernando Torres as a personal, non-commercial learning project using Azure Communication Services.

This site provides a public, consent-based opt-in and opt-out for SMS notifications using Azure Communication Services. Participation is voluntary and low-frequency. Opt out anytime by replying STOP.

Program Description

Participants (myself and a small group of consenting testers) may receive occasional SMS related to apps and prototypes I’m developing. Messages are limited in frequency and only sent with consent.

Example Messages

  • [SMS Test] Thanks for joining my personal SMS test program. Reply STOP to opt out. HELP for help.
  • [SMS Test] Sample alert for testing and product update purposes.
  • [SMS Test] System check completed at 3:00 PM.

Typical frequency: fewer than 10 messages per month. Message & data rates may apply. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
