Personal SMS Test Program

This page describes the opt-in process for Fernando Torres’ personal SMS learning project. The project uses Azure Communication Services to test SMS functionality. This is for development and testing only, not for commercial use.

Program Description

Participants may receive occasional SMS test messages while I develop applications. Recipients include myself and a small group of friends who have provided direct consent.

Example Messages

  • [Azure SMS Test] Thank you for joining my personal SMS test program. Reply STOP to opt out.
  • [Azure SMS Test] This is a sample notification for testing purposes.
  • [Azure SMS Test] Debug: App triggered SMS successfully at 3:00 PM.

Opt-In

To participate, you must provide your phone number directly to me. By doing so, you agree to receive occasional test SMS messages for this project.

Opt-Out

You may opt out at any time by notifying me. Once opted out, your number will be removed immediately and no further SMS will be sent.
