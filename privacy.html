Privacy Policy

Data Collected: Phone numbers and optional name/email provided via opt-in; opt-out requests. No payment or sensitive personal data is requested.

Purpose: To send occasional test SMS messages and maintain records of opt-in/opt-out for compliance and learning purposes.

Use & Sharing: Data is used only for the purposes above and is not sold or shared with third parties.

Storage: Submissions are logged to a private secure storage controlled by the program owner.

Retention: Data may be retained as needed for testing and compliance; you may request deletion at any time.

Your Choices: Reply STOP to any message to opt out. You can also use the web forms on the main site to opt in/out.

Contact: For access, correction, or deletion requests, email contact@sms-service-project.org.
