Data We Collect

  • Mobile phone number (required) and optional name/email submitted during opt-in; opt-out requests and related logs.
  • Basic technical logs from form submissions (e.g., timestamp, user agent) to maintain records of consent.

Purpose & Legal Basis

To send occasional test SMS messages for development/learning and to maintain records of consent and opt-out for compliance.

Use & Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We use processors solely to run this project, including Microsoft Azure Communication Services for SMS delivery and Azure storage/services for logging and hosting.

Retention

We keep data for as long as needed for testing/compliance or until you ask us to delete it. Opt-out requests are retained in a suppression log to honor future opt-outs.

Your Choices

  • Reply STOP to any message to opt out at any time.
  • Email contact@sms-service-project.org for assistance.
  • Email us to access, correct, or delete your data; we will respond within a reasonable time.

Security

We apply reasonable administrative and technical safeguards appropriate to a small non-commercial project (role-limited access, encrypted transport, and secure hosting).
