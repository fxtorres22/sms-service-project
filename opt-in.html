Opt-In to SMS Messages

This page provides a minimal, public description of how to opt in to the SMS Service Project by Fernando Torres (educational, non-commercial).

By providing your number, you consent to receive occasional test SMS messages for this learning project. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to end. For HELP with this test program, email contact@sms-service-project.org. See Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions. Frequency: under 10/month.

To actually request opt-in, please use the main site’s live form at https://www.sms-service-project.org/#opt-in.

Questions? contact@sms-service-project.org
