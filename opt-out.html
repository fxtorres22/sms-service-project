Opt-Out of SMS Messages

This page provides information on how to stop receiving SMS messages from the SMS Service Project by Fernando Torres.

How to Opt Out

  • Reply STOP to any message you receive.
  • You will receive a confirmation message: “You have successfully opted out and will receive no further messages.”
  • Alternatively, you can fill out the opt-out form on the main website at https://www.sms-service-project.org/#opt-out.
  • For assistance, reply HELP or email contact@sms-service-project.org..

Additional Information

Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency is typically under 10 per month. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
